About Me!

My name is Vickram Beharry, I'm a Guyanese and I was born on 21st March 1999. I grew up with my parents
in a village called Enmore of which I attended, 'Enmore Nursery School'.Upon graduting I then further attended
'Enmore Primary School' which I completed my entire primary educational life there. After graduting from 'Enmore Primary'
I moved on towards completing my secondary education at a school called 'Hope Secondary School'.

After my graduating for my secondary education, wasting little to no time I applied for several jobs,that of which turned out to be
no luck, so I did construction work with my father for several months,However I did not give up, after a prolong period of time I then
applied to go in the teaching profession,in just a month time, YES you guessed it, I was a Mathematics teacher at 'Beterverwagting Secondary
School'.

There I met the best set of people which told me to dream big an invest in my education,after working for several months I decided to go
further, but I didn't exactly know in what, but with th age of technology I just knew it had to be something with technology.So i finally
decided and now here I am. I am currently a second year student at the University of Guyana in the feild of Computer Science.

I'm a very interactive person who enjoy meeting new people. My hobbies are playing sports, being on social
media, reading novels, hanging with friends and anything that peaks my intrest.
